This Policy is provided for App Store compliance and user transparency. If product features, processors, or data flows change, this page will be updated promptly.

1. Who We Are

Averi is a personal AI assistant application that helps you analyze documents and information you provide (including, where you choose, health-related or medical documents), manage local workflow history, and optionally purchase memberships or AI credit packs through the Apple App Store.

Unless otherwise stated, “we”, “us”, and “our” refer to the individual developer of Averi (Averi 个人开发者), a personal developer based in the People’s Republic of China. “You” means the end user of Averi.

2. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains:

  • what information Averi collects and processes;
  • how that information is collected;
  • all uses of that information;
  • with whom it is shared (including third-party AI providers);
  • where processing occurs (on-device vs. servers);
  • your choices, consent, and rights;
  • how to contact us.

This Policy applies to the iOS version of Averi distributed via the Apple App Store. Apple’s own privacy practices are governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy.

3. Information We Process

3.1 Information you provide

  • Content you upload or enter: text, photos, camera captures, PDFs/documents, and other materials you submit to run a workflow or AI analysis.
  • Health-related content (only if you submit it): medical documents, lab/report images, medication lists, or similar materials. Averi is not a medical device and does not provide diagnosis or treatment. Such content is processed solely to provide the features you request.
  • Feedback: if you email support/feedback (e.g., to Averi@familymedvault.com), we receive the content of your message and any identifiers included by your email provider.

3.2 Information collected automatically on device

  • Local app data: workflow history, preferences (e.g., language), AI privacy consent preference, subscription/credit state stored locally, and similar settings needed for the app to function.
  • Device permissions you grant: camera, photo library, and document access are used only when you initiate the corresponding action. We do not access these permissions in the background for unrelated purposes.
  • Diagnostics (if enabled by the OS / App Store): crash or performance signals may be processed by Apple according to your device settings.

3.3 Purchase information

Memberships and AI credit packs are sold as Apple In-App Purchases. Payment card details are processed by Apple, not by us. We may receive limited commercial records from Apple necessary to unlock features (for example: product identifier, purchase/transaction status, and subscription period). We do not receive your full payment card number.

3.4 Information we do not intentionally collect

  • We do not require you to create an Averi account and password to use core local features.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use your content to build advertising profiles.
  • We do not request sensitive government ID numbers as a condition of using Averi.

4. How We Use Information

  • to provide, maintain, and improve Averi’s workflows and AI analysis features;
  • to transmit the content needed for an AI request to the recipients described in Section 5, after you grant permission;
  • to show remaining AI credits / membership status in the app;
  • to respond to support and feedback requests;
  • to comply with applicable laws, App Store rules, and enforce our EULA;
  • to protect the security and integrity of our services and users;
  • to establish, exercise, or defend legal claims.

Legal bases (where GDPR/UK GDPR or similar frameworks apply): performance of a contract, legitimate interests (secure and improve the service), consent (including in-app AI data-sharing permission and certain device permissions), and legal obligation.

5. AI Processing and Data Flow (Important)

Averi uses the Averi AI Gateway to run AI-powered workflows. Production builds do not require you to enter your own OpenAI or Gemini API keys in the app for cloud AI to work.

What data may be sent: the text and document content (or extracted text excerpts) that you provide for the specific workflow you run — only what is needed to complete that request. Payment card numbers are not sent to the AI gateway or model providers.

Who receives the data:

  • Averi AI Gateway (our backend AI gateway / processor), which securely receives the request; and
  • depending on the selected model, one of the following third-party AI providers acting as processors: OpenAI and/or Google Gemini (Google).

Permission before sharing: before Averi first shares your content with the Averi AI Gateway and these AI providers for analysis, the app presents an in-app disclosure and asks for your permission (Allow / Don’t Allow). You may review or withdraw AI data sharing later in Settings → AI Privacy → AI Data Sharing. If you do not allow AI data processing, Averi will not send that content to the Averi AI Gateway or to OpenAI / Google Gemini for the request.

  • We transmit only what is needed to fulfill your request.
  • Submitted content is used to generate the AI response for that request; it is not used for advertising and is not sold.
  • We require processors that receive personal data to provide protection that is the same as or equal to the protections described in this Policy, to the extent applicable to their role as processors.
  • Model providers may process data in jurisdictions outside your country of residence.
  • You should avoid submitting unnecessary highly sensitive identifiers (e.g., full national ID numbers) when possible; redact before upload if appropriate.

On-device storage helps keep your library and history convenient locally, but AI analysis is not purely on-device when remote AI is used. Statements that refer to “files staying on your device” describe local storage of your library where applicable; they do not mean that no data is ever transmitted during AI analysis.

6. Sharing and Recipients

We may share information only as follows:

  • Averi AI Gateway: to route and fulfill AI analysis requests you authorize.
  • OpenAI and/or Google Gemini (Google): third-party AI processors that generate model outputs for authorized requests, as described in Section 5.
  • Other service providers / processors: hosting, security, and email infrastructure under contractual or equivalent safeguards.
  • Apple: for distribution, In-App Purchase, analytics/crash reporting you enable, and App Store compliance.
  • Legal / safety: when required by law, court order, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business transfer: in a merger, acquisition, or reorganization, subject to continued protection consistent with this Policy or notice to you.

We do not sell personal information and do not share it for cross-app advertising.

7. Health-Related Content — Extra Caution

  • Health-related materials are processed only because you upload/input them.
  • If you run an AI workflow on health-related content after granting AI data-sharing permission, that content may be sent to the Averi AI Gateway and then to OpenAI or Google Gemini solely to fulfill your request.
  • Averi’s outputs are informational and educational aids only — not clinical advice.
  • Always consult qualified clinicians for medical decisions.
  • We do not use health-related content for advertising.
  • Where applicable laws treat health data as sensitive personal information, we process it only for the purpose you initiate and with your instructions/consent as required.

8. Data Retention

  • On-device data: retained until you delete it in-app, uninstall the app, or clear device storage.
  • AI privacy consent preference: stored locally on your device until you change it or clear app data.
  • Transient AI request data: retained only as long as needed to generate the response, operate/secure the service, debug abuse/fraud, and meet legal obligations; then deleted or anonymized in accordance with our retention practices.
  • Support emails: retained as needed to resolve your request and for legitimate business/legal records.
  • Purchase records: retained as required for accounting, tax, fraud prevention, and App Store reconciliation.

9. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures (including transport encryption for network requests where used, access controls, and least-privilege practices). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for controlling physical access to your device and OS passcode/biometrics.

10. International Transfers

If you use Averi from outside the processing location of our servers or model providers (including OpenAI and Google Gemini), your information may be transferred to and processed in other countries, including jurisdictions that may have different data-protection laws. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards (such as contractual protections) for such transfers.

11. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • access / copy personal information we hold about you;
  • correct inaccurate information;
  • delete information;
  • restrict or object to certain processing;
  • withdraw consent (without affecting prior lawful processing);
  • lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

AI data sharing: you can allow or deny AI data processing in the in-app consent prompt, and later change this in Settings → AI Privacy → AI Data Sharing. Turning AI Data Sharing off withdraws permission for future AI requests until you allow again.

In Averi, you can typically delete local history/files via in-app controls (where available) or by uninstalling the app. For other requests, email Averi@familymedvault.com. We may need to verify your request to protect against unauthorized access.

Device permissions: you may revoke camera/photos/files access in iOS Settings. Some features will stop working if permission is denied.

12. Children

Averi is not directed to children under 13 (or under 14 where required by local law, including relevant rules in Mainland China regarding minors). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below the applicable age. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take reasonable steps to delete it. Guardians in Mainland China should supervise minors’ use of AI and document features.

13. Third-Party Links and Services

Averi may reference third-party services (including Apple, OpenAI, and Google). Their privacy practices are controlled by those parties. Review their policies before use. For AI processing specifically, see Section 5.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy to reflect product, legal, or operational changes. The “Last Updated” date will be revised. Material changes will be indicated by updating this page and, where appropriate, in-app notice. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance to the extent permitted by law.

15. Governing Language

This English version and the Chinese version are provided for convenience. For App Store users worldwide, the English text may be used for interpretation when versions differ, except where mandatory local law (including Mainland China) requires otherwise for local consumers.

Averi 隐私政策（中文）

欢迎使用 Averi。本政策说明我们如何处理与 Averi（iOS，Bundle ID： com.jchen.Averi）相关的信息。 运营者为 Averi 个人开发者。 联系邮箱：Averi@familymedvault.com。

一、我们是谁

Averi 是一款个人 AI 助手应用，可在您主动提供内容的情况下，协助分析文档与信息 （含您自愿上传的健康/医疗相关材料），管理本地工作流记录，并可通过 Apple App Store 购买会员或 AI 额度包。Averi 不是医疗器械，不提供诊断或治疗建议。 本应用由中国境内的个人开发者开发与运营。

二、我们处理哪些信息

1. 您主动提供的信息

  • 文本、照片、相机拍摄内容、PDF/文档等，用于启动工作流或 AI 分析；
  • 您自愿提交的健康/医疗相关材料（仅因您上传/输入而处理）；
  • 您通过邮件发送的反馈/支持内容。

2. 设备本地自动产生的信息

  • 本地历史、语言偏好、AI 隐私授权偏好、订阅/额度状态等应用运行所需数据；
  • 仅在您触发功能时，经系统授权使用的相机、相册、文件访问权限；
  • 您在系统/App Store 设置中允许的崩溃或性能诊断（由 Apple 按其规则处理）。

3. 购买信息

会员与额度通过 Apple 应用内购买结算。支付卡信息由 Apple 处理，我们不会获得完整银行卡号。 我们可能从 Apple 获得开通功能所需的有限交易信息（如产品标识、交易状态、订阅周期等）。

4. 我们不会故意收集的内容

  • 不强制注册 Averi 账号密码即可使用主要本地功能；
  • 不出售个人信息；
  • 不将您的内容用于广告画像；
  • 不将提供身份证号等作为使用条件。

三、我们如何使用信息

  • 提供、维护与改进 Averi 功能与 AI 分析；
  • 在您授权后，将完成本次 AI 请求所需的内容传输至本政策第四节所述接收方；
  • 展示会员/AI 额度状态；
  • 回应支持与反馈；
  • 遵守法律法规与 App Store 规则，履行 EULA；
  • 保障安全、防控滥用，并依法维护合法权益。

四、AI 处理与数据传输（重要）

Averi 使用 Averi AI Gateway（Averi AI 网关）运行 AI 工作流。 正式版不要求您在 App 内自行填写 OpenAI 或 Gemini API 密钥才能使用云端 AI。

会发送哪些数据：您为当前工作流提供的文本、文档内容或提取后的文本摘要等， 且仅限于完成本次请求所必需的内容。支付卡号不会发送给 AI 网关或模型服务商。

数据发送给谁：

  • Averi AI Gateway（我们的后端 AI 网关 / 受托处理者），安全接收请求；以及
  • 根据所选模型，由以下第三方 AI 服务商作为受托处理者进行处理： OpenAI 和/或 Google Gemini（Google）。

发送前征得同意：在 Averi 首次将您的内容发送至 Averi AI Gateway 及上述 AI 服务商进行分析前， 应用内会展示说明并请求您的许可（允许 / 不允许）。您可稍后在 设置 → AI 隐私 → AI 数据共享中查看或撤回授权。 若您不允许 AI 数据处理，Averi 不会为该次请求向 Averi AI Gateway、OpenAI 或 Google Gemini 发送相关内容。

  • 我们仅传输完成请求所需的数据；
  • 提交内容仅用于生成本次 AI 结果，不用于广告，也不会出售；
  • 我们要求接收个人信息的受托方提供不低于本政策所述水平的保护（就其作为处理者的职责范围而言）；
  • 模型服务可能在境外处理数据；
  • 请尽量避免上传非必要的高度敏感身份信息，必要时请先脱敏。

AI 分析并不等于“全程仅在设备本地推理”。 应用内关于“文件保存在设备”的说明，指您的资料库/历史可本地保存； 不能理解为 AI 分析过程中绝不会发生网络传输。
