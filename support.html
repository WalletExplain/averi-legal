2. What Averi Is (and Is Not)

  • Averi is a personal AI assistant for document/workflow analysis and productivity.
  • Averi is not a medical device and does not provide diagnosis, treatment, or emergency care.
  • AI outputs may be incomplete or incorrect. Always verify important decisions with qualified professionals.
  • For medical emergencies, contact local emergency services immediately — do not rely on Averi.

3. Common Issues & Fixes

AI analysis failed / no result

  • Check network connectivity.
  • Retry with a shorter document or clearer photo.
  • Confirm you still have AI credits / an active membership (Subscription Center).
  • Force-quit Averi and reopen; update iOS and Averi if an update is available.

Cannot upload photo / PDF / camera

  • Go to iOS Settings → Averi and allow Camera / Photos / Files access.
  • Re-try from the workflow input screen after granting permission.

Language / UI looks wrong

  • Switch language in Averi settings (Chinese / English), then reopen the screen.

Local history or files missing

  • Uninstalling Averi or clearing app data deletes local content.
  • We cannot recover deleted on-device data from email support.

4. Subscriptions, Credits & Refunds (Apple)

All payments for Averi memberships and AI credit packs are processed by Apple via In-App Purchase. The individual developer of Averi does not receive your card details and cannot manually refund App Store charges.

  • Manage or cancel subscriptions: Apple — View, change, or cancel subscriptions
  • Request a refund: reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Purchase history: Apple — See your purchase history

If a purchase did not unlock features after Apple confirms payment, email us with the approximate purchase time and product name (e.g., Personal Monthly / Family Yearly / 500 AI Credits). We can help investigate entitlement issues, but billing disputes are handled by Apple.

三、常见问题

AI 分析失败 / 无结果

  • 检查网络；
  • 换更清晰的照片或更短文档重试；
  • 确认订阅中心仍有 AI 额度或有效会员；
  • 强制退出后重开；更新 iOS 与 Averi。

无法拍照 / 选图 / 上传 PDF

  • 到 iOS「设置 → Averi」打开相机 / 照片 / 文件权限后重试。

语言显示异常

  • 在 Averi 设置中切换中文/英文后重新进入页面。

本地记录丢失

  • 卸载应用或清理数据会导致本地内容删除；技术支持无法从邮件端恢复已删除的本地数据。
